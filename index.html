Our Story

A Love Story
Baked Into Every Roti

It started with a grandmother who believed that the heart of a home lives in its kitchen. Three generations ago, our family set up a small stone chakki in Pune, grinding the finest wheat with patience, love, and an unshakeable commitment to purity.

Today, Sai Prasad Chakki Fresh Aata is still made the same way — slowly, carefully, and with deep love. We source wheat from trusted farmers, stone-grind it fresh, and pack it without a single additive or preservative.

Because when your family sits together over soft, warm rotis — that moment deserves nothing but the very best.

— The Sai Prasad Family 🌾

Our Promise

The Sai Prasad Promise —
Because Your Family Deserves the Best

We don't cut corners. We don't take shortcuts. Every grain that goes into your home has been chosen, cleaned, and ground with absolute care.

🪨

Stone Ground Process

Traditional chakki stone grinding preserves natural nutrients, oils, and flavour that high-speed industrial mills destroy. Slower grinding, better atta — it's that simple.

🚜

Farm-to-Pack Freshness

We work directly with trusted farmers from Punjab and Madhya Pradesh. Our wheat travels from field to your kitchen with minimal handling and maximum freshness.

🌿

Zero Additives, Always

No bleaching. No maida mixing. No preservatives. No shortcuts. Just pure, honest wheat flour — exactly as nature intended it to be.
